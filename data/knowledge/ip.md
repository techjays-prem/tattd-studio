curator: tattd-studio-dev

synthetic: true
permission: synthetic-content-tattd-studio-poc
---

US case law treats original tattoo designs as the artist's copyrighted work (see *Whitmill v. Warner Bros.*, 2011 — the Mike Tyson facial tattoo in *The Hangover Part II*). Studios should clarify in client agreements whether the artist retains rights, grants the client a license, or transfers ownership.

In the US, works enter the public domain 70 years after the author's death (life+70). Pre-1929 published works are public domain. Country-by-country rules differ; for international clients verify under the destination jurisdiction.
